Publications

  • Functional Random Forests for Curve Response

    Scientific Reports · Dec 17, 2021

    Introduces a novel functional random forests (FunFor) approach to model functional data response that is densely and regularly measured. The FunFor approach predicts curve responses for new observations and selects important variables from a large set of scalar predictors. It inherits the efficiency of traditional random forests in detecting complex relationships, including nonlinear and high-order interactions, without imposing parametric and distributional assumptions. An R package named 'FunFor' implementing the approach is available on GitHub.
